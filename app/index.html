YOUR MOTIVATION TO COOK

Users of recipes apps often find it difficult to get all ingredients listed in recipes. This greatly discourages them from using such apps.

Our solution is to have ingredients always ready for cook by two major methods:

  • Match recipes with on-hand ingredients using advance search
  • Get ingredients easily and instantly by online ordering
